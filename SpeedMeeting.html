Speed Meeting

Our Responsibilities

In this group project, we with the academic administration to secure venue arrangements.

After persistent negotiations, we successfully secured the use of the "Sunlight Hall" located on the top floor of our college building - an ideal space fully equipped to support the Speed Meeting event.
Our team also contributed to on-site setup by establishing a dedicated "Guest Area" and multiple discussion zones, creating an efficient environment for productive exchanges between faculty and students.

Key Takeaways

Professor Yang Guang's Wisdom: The esteemed senior professor emphasized holistic development: "While pursuing academic excellence and technical mastery, one must equally cultivate interpersonal skills and develop strategic vision. True achievement comes from balanced growth that makes your capabilities visible to others."

Professor Yu Hongbo's Encouragement: Our department chair offered invaluable motivation: "Maintain confidence in your abilities, your discipline, and our institution. Embrace challenges outside your comfort zone, especially public speaking. Forcing yourself to practice these essential skills is the only way to grow."
